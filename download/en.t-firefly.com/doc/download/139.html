ITX-3588J Buy Specs

Powered by Rockchip RK3588 new-gen 8-core 64-bit processor, the mainboard can be configured with up to 32GB RAM. Capable of 8K video encoding and decoding, it provides abundant interfaces supporting multiple hard disks, Gigabit Ethernet, WiFi6, 5G/4G expansion and a variety of video input and output. It supports different power supply ways and various operating systems.
